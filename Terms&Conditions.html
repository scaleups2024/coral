Terms & Conditions

1. Identity Proof Requirements

As per Government regulations, all guests (single, double, or triple occupancy) must present a valid photo ID at check-in.

Foreign Nationals:

  • Valid Passport
  • Visa

Indian Nationals: Acceptable government-issued photo ID with address proof:

  • Passport
  • Aadhar Card
  • Voter’s ID
  • Driving License

Note: PAN Cards are not accepted.

Corporate Bookings: Please carry proof of corporate affiliation at check-in.

2. Guarantee Policy

All reservations must be guaranteed at the time of booking with a valid credit card. We accept all major credit cards.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • Reservations must be cancelled at least 72 hours before check-in.
  • Cancellations within 72 hours will incur one night’s charge.
  • Promotional offers (100% advance) are non-cancellable, non-refundable, and non-amendable.

4. Refund Policy

Refunds are processed as per the cancellation/no-show policy and will be returned to the same credit card used for booking.

  • Usually reflected in the next billing cycle.
  • May take up to 30–45 business days, depending on your bank.
